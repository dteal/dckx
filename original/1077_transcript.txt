n\nThis comic is a take on the typical "how to" which details "how to" organize your home. In many cases, finding the best organization can be difficult and/or can take a long time. To skip this problem, Cueball "Just Gives Up" and puts all his items and furniture into a box labeled  "Misc" for miscellaneous, with the exception of his laptop, cable modem and router.\nThe image text "Lifehacking" is a take on the popular website Lifehacker which includes all sorts of posts on how to "hack" your life and improve your life.\n\n

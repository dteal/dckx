n\nRecycling plants often can only process one type of material at a time, which necessitates some form of sorting facility. Randall show some degree of thoughtfulness in separating his paper from metals for the convenience of the people working at the facility, but then he leaves congealed juice in a bottle, rendering it unrecyclable without cleaning and extra effort on the part of the recycling facility.\nThe title text implies that the workers know his address, most likely from various envelopes and junk mail that he has recycled.\nAs always, Randall is both a self-conscious man and a paranoid man.\n\n

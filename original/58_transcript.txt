n\nCueball asks "Why do you love me?" to Megan, a fairly common question that couples ask each other. She responds "My heart never gave me a choice", a seemingly very sentimental, romantic answer. However, after a beat panel she effectively kills the romance of the moment by adding, "I wish it had", indicating that she would rather not have loved Cueball.\nScott appears to be a friend of Randall Munroe.
